Samsung Galaxy A53 5G is a decent smartphone for most consumers – it packs an Exynos 1280 Octa-Core CPU. It can run most apps as it has a 6GB or 8GB of RAM and 128GB or 256GB of storage. Samsung Galaxy A53 5G‘s quad camera setup has an impressive 64MP camera sensor. The 5000 mAh battery also comes with a 25W Fast charging, just like the other phones in the A series/lineup.It is available in 3 variants: ₱22,990 for the Online Exclusive, which comes with 6GB/128GB, ₱23,490 for the 8GB/128GB, and ₱25,490 for the 8GB/256GB of ram and internal storage.
