Huawei nova 10

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Snapdragon 778G 4G

  • RAM: 8GB

  • STORAGE: 256GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.67 in 1080P OLED 120Hz

  • CAMERA: 50+8+2MP rear / 60MP front

  • OS: EMUI 12

  • BATTERY: 4000mAh Li-Po, 66W SuperCharge


The Huawei nova 10 is a high-end mid-range smartphone with a 60MP front camera that records 4K video, triple back cameras that are 50MP + 8MP + 2MP, and a 6.7-inch curved-edge FHD+ OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate.It is powered by an EMUI 12 operating system and a Qualcomm Snapdragon 778G CPU with 8GB of RAM. A fingerprint scanner hidden beneath the display, dual-SIM 4G LTE connectivity, Wi-Fi 6, NFC, stereo twin speakers, and 256GB of internal storage are also included.Huawei nova 10 is powered by a 4,000 mAh battery and 66W HUAWEI SuperCharge technology. There are three color options available: Provence, Starry Black, and Starry Silver. It has a “Star Orbit” camera ring design and is 6.88 mm thin.The Huawei nova 10 has an official pricing of ₱26,999 which comes with 8GB/256GB of ram and internal storage configuration.
