The Infinix Hot 11 2022 runs on an Android 11 operating system, an XOS 10 Lite interface, and a UNISOC T610 chipset with 4GB of RAM. There’s likewise a side-mounted unique finger impression scanner, double SIM 4G LTE network, sound system double speakers, and 64GB of expandable storage. The device is powered by a large 5000 mAh battery that can be charged at a 10W rate via USB type-C. The Infinix Hot 11 2022 is priced at ₱5,799 for the 4/64GB configuration.
