TCL 30 SE

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Helio G25

  • RAM: 4GB

  • STORAGE: 128GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.52 in 720p IPS LCD 60Hz

  • CAMERA: 50 + 2 + 2MP rear / 8MP front

  • OS: TCL UI 4.0, Android 12

  • BATTERY: 5000mAh Li-ion


With a 6.52-inch HD+ display, 50MP + 2MP + 2MP triple rear cameras, and an 8MP front camera, the TCL 30 SE is a cost-effective smartphone. It is powered by an Android 12 OS, a 4GB RAM MediaTek Helio G25 CPU, and a TCL UI 4.0 user interface. Additionally, there is a fingerprint scanner positioned on the back, dual-band Wi-Fi, 4G LTE connectivity, and 128GB of expandable storage. The smartphone is powered by a sizable 5000mAh battery and supports 15W fast charging through USB Type-C. The TCL 30 SE smartphone has a ₱4,990 official price in the Philippines. It is currently offered in brick-and-mortar stores and online stores run by TCL.
