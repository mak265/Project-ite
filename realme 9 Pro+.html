The realme 9 Pro+ is powered by a MediaTek Dimensity 920 chipset, 8GB of RAM, has a vapor chamber cooling system, runs Android 12 OS, with realme UI 3.0 on top. There’s also a fingerprint scanner under the display, 5G connectivity, dual-band Wi-Fi, NFC, Dolby ATMOS audio, stereo speakers, and offers a 256GB of internal storage.The device is powered by a 4500mAh battery that supports 60W SuperDart fast charging technology.It is available for ₱23,990 which comes with 8GB/128GB of ram and internal storage.
