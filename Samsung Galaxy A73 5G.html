The Samsung Galaxy A73 5G is one of Samsung’s newest offerings under the mid-range line. It’s available in either 6GB or 8GB of RAM with 128GB or 256GB of internal storage. It is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 778G 5G processor.The Samsung Galaxy A73 5G‘s 6.7 Super AMOLED+ display makes it for a rich viewing experience paired with the large screen size. Under its hood, it has the Snapdragon 778G 5G processor that will keep you covered in any multitasking.Starting at around Php26,990 for the 6GB/128GB variant, through an online exclusive on Samsung’s website.
