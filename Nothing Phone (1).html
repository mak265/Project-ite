Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines

Nothing Phone (1) is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 778G+ chipset with 8GB of RAM, runs on Android 12 operating system with Nothing OS software on top.It has a 6.55-inch FHD+ OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, dual rear cameras with 50MP + 50MP OIS, and a single 16MP selfie camera. There’s also an under-display fingerprint scanner, 5G connectivity, dual stereo speakers, and internal storage options of 128GB or 256GB.The device is powered by a 4,500 mAh battery that supports 33W fast charging and 15W wireless charging.Nothing Phone (1) is available for ₱27,990 for the 8/128GB, ₱29,990 for the 8GB/256GB, and ₱32,990 for the 12GB/256GB of ram and internal storage.
