Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Dimensity 920

  • STORAGE: 256GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.44 in 1080P AMOLED 90Hz, HDR10+

  • CAMERA: 64+8+2MP rear / 50+8MP front

  • OS: Android 11, FunTouch OS 12

  • BATTERY: 4200 mAh, 44W FlashCharge


The vivo V23 5G is powered by MediaTek Dimensity 920 chipset, 12GB of RAM, 4GB of extended RAM, Android 12 OS, and Funtouch OS 12 software. There’s also a fingerprint scanner under the display, dual-SIM 5G connectivity, NFC, Hi-Res audio, and 256GB of internal storage. It is powered by a 4,200 mAh battery that supports 44W fast charging technology. vivo V23 5G is available for ₱27,999 which comes with 8GB/256GB of ram and internal storage.
