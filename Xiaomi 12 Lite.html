Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Snapdragon 778G 5G

  • DISPLAY: 6.55 in 1080P AMOLED 120Hz, HDR10+

Xiaomi 12 Lite is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 778G chipset with either 6GB or 8GB of RAM, runs on Android 12 OS, with MIUI 13 interface. There’s also dual-SIM 5G connectivity, an under-display fingerprint scanner, Wi-Fi 6, NFC, also features an infrared remote control function, Dolby ATMOS audio, and 128GB of internal storage.It is powered by a 4,300mAh battery with 67W fast-charging technology via USB type-C.Xiaomi 12 Lite is available for ₱20,999 with 6GB/128GB and ₱21,999 for the 8GB/128GB.
