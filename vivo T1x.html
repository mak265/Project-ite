vivo T1x runs on Qualcomm Snapdragon 680 Octa-core CPU and also has 1080P IPS LCD display with 90Hz of screen refresh rate. It also runs on vivo’s own software, Funtouch OS 12 based on Android 12. Other specs also include: 50MP main camera, a large 5000 mAh battery coupled with 18W Fast Charge and a 4GB of RAM and 64GB of internal storage. The vivo T1x is focused more on to the gaming side of things and is priced at ₱8,999 for the 4GB/64GB.
