Tecno Pova Neo 2

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Helio G85

  • RAM: 6GB

  • STORAGE: 128GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.8 in 720p IPS LCD 90Hz

  • CAMERA: 16 + 2MP rear / 8MP front

  • OS: HiOS 8.6, Android 12

  • BATTERY: 7000mAh Li-Po


Tecno’s upcoming smartphone, Tecno Pova Neo 2, will now showcase a different design and camera modules with two rear cameras, and a single front one. Tecno Pova Neo 2‘s 6.8-inch HD+ display that’s also in 1640 x 720 resolution made from IPS LCD, and 263 ppi makes this an indoor type-smartphone due to low brightness it has. However, the 90Hz refresh rate makes up for it. The MediaTek Helio G85 is a decent choice for Tecno Pova Neo 2, but still can’t quite cut it when it comes to gaming and multitasking. When it comes to battery management, you can never go wrong with the Tecno Pova Neo 2’s 7,000mAh battery with 18W fast charging. You can choose from these three colors: Virtual Blue, Uranolite Gray, and our favorite: Orange Magma. The smartphone looks like a rugged phone from afar but upon closer inspection, it’s just a really intricate design. The 6/128 variant is priced around PHP 6,999 and is now available in the Philippines.
