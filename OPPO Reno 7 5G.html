The OPPO Reno 7 5G is powered by MediaTek Dimensity 900 chipset with 8GB of RAM, has a multi-cooling system, runs on Android 11 OS with ColorOS 12 software on top. There’s also a fingerprint scanner under the display, 5G connectivity, Wi-Fi 6 support, NFC, and 256GB of expandable storage.It is also powered by a 4,500 mAh Li-Po dual-battery system that supports 65W SUPERVOOC fast charging technology.OPPO Reno 7 5G retails for ₱26,999 which comes with 8GB/256GB of ram and internal storage.
