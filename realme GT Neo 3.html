The realme GT Neo 3 has a racing-inspired dual stripe design, a flagship-grade MediaTek Dimensity 8100 5G chipset, a stainless steel vapor chamber for cooling, and an 80W SuperDart ultra-fast charging technology.It has a 6.7-inch FHD+ AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate, three rear cameras with Sony IMX766 main camera sensors, and a 16MP punch-hole selfie camera. There’s also a fingerprint scanner under the display, dual 5G connectivity, NFC, Wi-Fi 6, Dolby ATMOS audio, and dual stereo speakers.realme GT Neo 3 is available for ₱29,999 which come with an 8GB/256GB of ram and internal storage configuration.
