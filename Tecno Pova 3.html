Tecno Pova 3

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Helio G88

  • RAM: 6GB

  • STORAGE: 128GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.9 in 1080p IPS LCD 90hz

  • CAMERA: 50+2+2MP rear / 6MP front

  • OS: Android Version: Android 12 OS: HiOS 8

  • BATTERY: 7000 mAh, 25W Fast Charging


The TECNO POVA 3 has a tall 6.9-inch FHD+ display with a 90Hz refresh rate, triple rear cameras of 50MP + 2MP + 2MP, and an 8MP front-facing camera. It is powered by an Android 12 OS, HiOS 8 software, and a MediaTek Helio G88 CPU with 6GB of RAM. Additionally, there are dual speakers, a side-mounted fingerprint scanner, dual-band Wi-Fi, 4G LTE connectivity, and 128GB of expandable storage. The device is powered by a huge 7,000mAh battery, and a 33W fast charger is provided in the box (the actual charging rate is 25W). TECNO POVA 3’s official price in the Philippines is ₱7,999 pesos for the Black/Silver model and ₱8,999 for the Electric Blue model
