HONOR X7

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Snapdragon 680 4G

  • RAM: 4GB

  • STORAGE: 128GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.74 in 720p IPS LCD 90Hz

  • CAMERA: 48 + 5 + 2 + 2MP rear / 8MP front

  • OS: Magic UI 4.2, Android 11

  • BATTERY: 5000 mAh


The Honor X7 is an entry-level smartphone offering from the former Huawei sub-brand, Honor. It has the Snapdragon 680 4G processor with an Adreno 610 GPU that’s been always fine with handling some moderate gaming and multitasking. As for the RAM, you can choose between 4GB or 6GB and 128GB internal storage. The Honor X7 has a quad camera setup for its rear, and a single front camera that are able to capture above-decent photos. As for its battery, a 5,000mAh Li-Po capacity will keep you company throughout the day. The Honor X7 has been released globally way back in March 2022, but as of Honor’s huge comeback in the PH market, it is expected to pop-up in the Magic launch event this September 27, and it is priced at ₱9,990 for 4/128GB.
