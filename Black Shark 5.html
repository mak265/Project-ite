Black Shark 5

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Snapdragon 870

  • RAM: 8/12GB

  • STORAGE: 128/256GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.67 in 1080P AMOLED 144Hz, HDR10+

  • CAMERA: 64+8+5MP rear / 20MP front

  • OS: Android 12, Joy UI 13

  • BATTERY: 4650 mAh, 120W HyperCharge


Black Shark 5 is Xiaomi’s dedicated gaming phone. It is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 870, paired with an either 8 or 12GB of ram, runs on Android 12 with JoyUI 12 on top that has extra “gaming-centric” software features. For the gamers out there, this phone also flaunts a magnetic pop-up triggers for your gaming needs.It sports a 4650 mAh battery which supports 120W HyperCharge via USB Type-C. Black Shark 5 is available for ₱26,999 with 8GB/128GB and ₱34,999 for the 12GB/256GB of ram and internal storage.
