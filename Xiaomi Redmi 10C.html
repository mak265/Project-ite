Xiaomi Redmi 10C

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Snapdragon 680

  • RAM: 4GB

  • STORAGE: 64GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.71 in 720p IPS LCD

  • CAMERA: 50+2MP rear / 5MP front

  • OS: Android 11 with MIUI 13

  • BATTERY: 5000 mAh, 18W Fast Charging


A budget-friendly smartphone with a 6.71-inch HD+ display, dual back cameras of 50MP + 2MP, and a single 5MP selfie camera is the Xiaomi Redmi 10C. It is powered by an Android 11 OS, a Qualcomm Snapdragon 680 CPU, and MIUI 13 software. Additionally, there is a fingerprint scanner, dual-band Wi-Fi, 4G LTE connectivity, and 64GB or 128GB of expandable storage. The Xiaomi Redmi 10C is powered by a sizable 5000mAh battery and supports 18W fast charging over USB Type-C. The Xiaomi Redmi 10C has an official pricing of ₱6,299 for the 64GB variant and ₱7,299 for the 128GB variant in the Philippines.
