An entry-level smartphone, the Infinix Hot 12i has a 6.6-inch HD+ display, a triple 13MP rear camera system, and an 8MP selfie camera. A fingerprint scanner located on the back, 4G LTE connectivity, DTS audio, and 64GB of expandable storage are all included. The Hot 12i has a sizable 5000mAh battery and 18W fast charging. The official pricing for the Infinix Hot 12i is ₱5,490.
