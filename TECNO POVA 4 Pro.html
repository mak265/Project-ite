TECNO POVA 4 Pro

Full Specs and Official Price in the Philippines



  • CPU: Helio G99

  • RAM: 8GB

  • STORAGE: 256GB

  • DISPLAY: 6.66 in 1080p AMOLED 90Hz

  • CAMERA: 50 + 2MP rear / 8MP front

  • OS: Android 12, HiOS 8.6

  • BATTERY: 6000mAh Li-Po, 45W Fast Charge


TECNO POVA 4 Pro is the fourth incarnation of the budget-level gaming smartphone from Tecno. It uses the new Helio G99 chipset with 8GB of RAM and 256GB internal storage.With a large 6.66 AMOLED display and in 90Hz, the TECNO POVA 4 Pro truly aims for the gamer market with these specifications. Not to mention, it has both expandable memory up to 1TB and expandable RAM up to 5GB. A massive 6,000mAh battery seems a departure from the POVA 3’s 7,000mAh though.As for the cameras, a combination of 50MP + 2MP for the rear and 8MP selfie camera might not seem much, but its the best for its price range. The TECNO POVA 4 Pro is priced at ₱9,699 SRP (₱8,999 promo price) for the 8/128 base variant and ₱9,699 at launch (from its ₱10,290 SRP) for its 8/256 variant.
